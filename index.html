Who I Am

About Me

I am a master student researcher at Kumoh National Institute of Technology, where I focus on UAV Perception and Learning. My work spans Machine Learning, Reinforcement Learning, Computer Vision, and Robotics, with the goal of developing methods that can be applied in real-world scenarios.

Prior to my current position, I completed my Bachelor of Computer Engineering at Telkom University, where I worked on SLAM and Navigation for Autonomous Mobile Robots with collaboration UAV-UGV. I have collaborated with APTRG-Telkom University and Shannon Dynamics where I worked on Robot Learning and Autonomous Collaborative UAV-UGV missions.
